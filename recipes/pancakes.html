Pancakes Recipe :

These fluffy pancakes are soft, light, and delicious.

They are perfect for breakfast or served with maple syrup, honey, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients :

  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 250 ml milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps :

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk together the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  4. Stir until just combined.
  5. Heat a lightly greased frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan.
  7. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface.
  8. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown.
  9. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  10. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
