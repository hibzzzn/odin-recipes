Cookies Recipe :

These homemade cookies are soft on the inside, slightly crispy on the edges, and full of rich buttery flavor.

They are perfect as a snack, dessert, or treat with a glass of milk or a cup of coffee.

Ingredients :

  • 125 g butter (softened)
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder (or ½ tsp baking soda)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 100 g chocolate chips (optional)

Steps :

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a large bowl until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then mix until well combined.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, and salt) in a separate bowl.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until a soft dough forms
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips if using.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper, leaving about 5 cm (2 inches) of space between each cookie.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  9. Let the cookies cool on the baking tray for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
