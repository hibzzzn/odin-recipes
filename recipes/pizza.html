Pizza Recipe :

This homemade pizza has a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese.

It is perfect for lunch, dinner, or sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients :

  • 250 g pizza dough
  • 100 ml tomato sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pepperoni or vegetables (optional)

Steps :

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Place the dough on a baking tray.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Add your favorite toppings.
  7. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil.
  9. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  10. Slice and serve hot.
